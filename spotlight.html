DM DOKURO


As this is the first spotlight I have chosen one of my own favourite artists and I think he does fit the 'small' criteria.
DM DOKURO is a solo music producer from the USA, who primarily focuses on metal, rock, and electronic music.
He is primarily known for his early undertale remixes as well as his slightly more recent work on the Calamity mod for the game Terraria.
